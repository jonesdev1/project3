Homelessness is a pressing issue in New Jersey, fueled by economic struggles, a shortage of affordable housing, and mental health concerns. Despite ongoing efforts by the state and nonprofits, challenges persist due to limited resources, resulting in overcrowded shelters and insufficient support services. Continued advocacy, increased funding, and community awareness are essential to address and alleviate homelessness in the state. You can make a positive impact by supporting your local shelters through donations and Volunteering.
